What to know about dogs

Dogs are known to be man's best friend.

There is a lot to know about dogs, here are some interesting and amazing facts about them;

  • Dogs have a sense of time.
  • Dogs are as smart as two-year olds.
  • Dogs can genuinely get jealous.
  • Some dog breeds were mainly bred for the purpose of hunting, e.g. dachshunds.
  • In Russia, dogs are said to use transport to go to specific places in search of food.
  • Dogs can smell feelings.
  • The Newfoundland dog breed has water-resistant coats and webbed feet making them great lifeguards.
  • Dalmatian dog breeds are born white and develop their black spots as they grow.
  • Petting a dog can help with our physical and mental health.
  • Dogs have wet noses to help them absorb scent chemicals.
